The Story of Tuku

Tuku is more than just a place on a map. It's a town woven from threads of history, culture, and the unforgettable aroma of sesame oil. From bustling markets to serene temples, every corner holds a story. Tuku GO is your personal guide to unlocking these treasures. We invite you to wander, to taste, and to discover the living history of this remarkable town.
